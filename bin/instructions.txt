instructions for biball:
when you hold down a key, you can see a number at the top-left. that number will be referred to x in explanation.
for example, if the counter reached 123 before i let go of a key, x would be 123.

when you hold down any of the arrow keys, the balls will be thrusted in the direction by x pixels.
when you press space, it spawns 1 ball.
when you hold enter, it spawns x balls.
when you press R, it resets the simulation and show how many balls you got.
when you press ESC, it quits the game and also shows how many balls you got.

NOTE: creating balls is also an automatic process, just press nothing and every 50 bounces a new ball will be created
